01Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier for your account and, depending on the permissions you grant during sign-in, your email address. We do not receive your Apple or Google password. You separately choose a username, which is not required to match your real name.

Content you post. The text of thoughts and comments you submit, along with the time you posted them.

Activity data used to operate the App. This includes:

  • Which thoughts you've bookmarked, and which users you've saved or blocked
  • A record of which posts you've viewed, used only to calculate accurate, non-duplicated read counts
  • Reports you file against content, including your account identifier as the reporter
  • The date you accepted our Terms of Use

Notification preferences. Whether you've enabled the daily reminder notification, and if so, the time it's scheduled for. This is stored on your device and, if applicable, in your account settings.

Technical information. Standard technical data processed automatically by our backend infrastructure (Google Firebase), such as device type and general diagnostic logs used to keep the App running reliably. We do not currently use separate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

02What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not collect payment information (the App does not currently process payments).
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks, and we do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

03How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

  • Operate core features of the App (posting, commenting, bookmarking, blocking, read counts)
  • Maintain the privacy protections described in our Terms of Use — for example, using your saved-users list only to personalize what "Shake Thots" shows you, never to reveal that information to anyone else
  • Review and act on reports of objectionable content
  • Send you the daily reminder notification, if you've enabled it
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the App

04Who We Share Information With

Google Firebase hosts our backend (authentication, database, and related infrastructure) and processes data on our behalf as a service provider. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

We may disclose information if required by law, or to investigate or address violations of our Terms of Use, including cases involving safety, fraud, or illegal activity.

05Your Choices and Rights

  • Editing or deleting your posts: available directly within the App under "My Thots."
  • Signing out: available within the App at any time.
  • Deleting your account and associated data entirely: contact us at thots.support@gmail.com.
  • Turning off notifications: manage this through your device's system notification settings for the App.

If you are located in a region with specific data protection rights (such as the EU/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA), you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete your data — contact us at the email above to make a request.

06Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data and posted content within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

07Children's Privacy

Thots is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at thots.support@gmail.com and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

08Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices provided by our infrastructure provider (Google Firebase), including encrypted data transmission and access-controlled databases. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

09International Users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States, depending on our infrastructure provider's data center locations.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to thots.support@gmail.com.
